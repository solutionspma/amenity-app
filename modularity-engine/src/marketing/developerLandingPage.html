What Is Modularity?

Modularity is a full AR/VR spatial environment for spiritual gatherings, education, small groups, creator studios, and interactive community engagement.

We're building the future of faith-based immersive experiences, combining cutting-edge XR technology with meaningful spiritual community features.

Built With

  • WebXR - Universal cross-platform XR support
  • Babylon.js - High-performance 3D rendering engine
  • Supabase real-time - Live synchronization and database
  • AI Avatar systems - Intelligent virtual presence
  • Spatial audio - Immersive 3D sound environments
  • AR hit-testing - Real-world integration
  • VR locomotion - Natural movement and navigation
  • Room-based 3D environments - Purpose-built spiritual spaces
  • Creator tools for churches - Empower faith communities
